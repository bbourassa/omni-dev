ATHLETIC PERFORMANCE PROGRAMS

Whether you are an athlete looking to take the next step in your career to college or the professional level, or you are a younger athlete looking to make the varsity team at your high school we work with you to optimize your performance. Along with building the necessary athletic qualities of strength, speed and explosiveness we will reduce your risk of injury, teach you proper exercise technique and how to rise to the top of your sport. Along with developing your athletic ability, at Omni Gym you will develop confidence, self esteem, and grow to be leaders on your team, in your school, and community. By investing your time and energy at Omni Gym you will create lifelong habits in exercise and nutrition that will go beyond your career as an athlete. Most of all you will have FUN doing it!
