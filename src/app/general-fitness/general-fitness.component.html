General Fitness Programs

At Omni Gym we strive to get you in the best shape possible. If you are looking to lose a few pounds, add some muscle, and get in better shape to do the things you love to do, we are here for you. Our general fitness programs are designed to not only help you look and feel the way you want, but to live longer and healthier.

SMALL GROUP TRAINING

Small Group Training is fun and challenging! You get the attention of a trainer who is able to cater to your individual needs, with the energy and support of a group. Our groups are anywhere from 2-6 people, so if you need a modification or a little more attention we got you covered! No more getting lost in the shuffle of a bootcamp.

PERSONAL TRAINING

Personal training includes a customized program based upon what YOU need. We will assess your status and After your initial assessment where we will go through your injury history and training history, we will help you set appropriate short and long term goals. You will work with a trainer one on one to get the results you deserve.
