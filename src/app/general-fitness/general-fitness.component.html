General Fitness Programs

PERSONAL TRAINING

At Omni Gym we strive to treat you as an individual, from the intake process through the completion of your workouts we keep you, and your goals in mind.
Personal training includes a customized program based upon what YOU need. After your initial assessment where we will go through your injury history and training history, we will help you set appropriate short and long term goals. You will work with a trainer one on one to get the results you deserve.

SEMI-PRIVATE TRAINING

Semi-Private Training has all of the same customized benefits as personal training, inside of a small group atmosphere. Working alongside others delivers the energetic feel and support of a group while working on a program unique to you and your goals.

GROUP FITNESS

Group fitness is a fun, cost effective way to train in an energetic group setting, while receiving a personal touch, with no assessment necessary. Regardless of your history you are welcome to train at your own pace inside of our welcoming and supportive environment.
